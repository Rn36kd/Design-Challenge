Exercise Routines

Routine 1: Full-Body Strength (Beginner-Friendly)

  • Squats – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups (knee or full) – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets of 10 reps (each arm)
  • Plank – 3 rounds of 20-30 seconds
  • Jumping Jacks – 3 sets of 20 reps

Routine 2: Cardio Blast (HIIT)

  • High Knees – 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
  • Burpees – 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
  • Jump Squats – 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
  • Mountain Climbers – 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
  • Rest 1 minute, repeat for 3-4 rounds

Routine 3: Core Strength

  • Plank – 3 rounds of 45 seconds
  • Russian Twists – 3 sets of 20 reps (10 per side)
  • Bicycle Crunches – 3 sets of 15 reps (each side)
  • Leg Raises – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Side Plank – 2 rounds of 30 seconds per side

Routine 4: Lower Body Focus

  • Lunges – 3 sets of 12 reps (each leg)
  • Deadlifts (bodyweight or dumbbells) – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Step-Ups (on a sturdy surface) – 3 sets of 10 reps (each leg)
  • Glute Bridges – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Calf Raises – 3 sets of 20 reps

Routine 5: Upper Body Strength

  • Push-ups – 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Tricep Dips (on a chair) – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bicep Curls – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Side Lateral Raises – 3 sets of 10 reps
