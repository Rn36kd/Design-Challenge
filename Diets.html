Diets

1. Balanced Diet Breakfast: Oatmeal with fresh berries, almond milk, and a boiled egg. Snack: Greek yogurt with a handful of nuts. Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, quinoa, and steamed broccoli. Snack: Apple slices with peanut butter. Dinner: Baked salmon, sweet potato, and a side of spinach. 2. Low-Carb Diet Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and avocado. Snack: Cheese cubes with cucumber slices. Lunch: Grilled steak with zucchini noodles and a salad. Snack: Hard-boiled egg with a handful of almonds. Dinner: Roast chicken thighs, sautéed mushrooms, and asparagus. 3. Vegan Diet Breakfast: Smoothie with almond milk, spinach, banana, and chia seeds. Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus. Lunch: Lentil curry with brown rice and steamed vegetables. Snack: Trail mix with dried fruit and nuts. Dinner: Tofu stir-fry with mixed vegetables and quinoa. 4. Mediterranean Diet Breakfast: Greek yogurt with honey, walnuts, and sliced figs. Snack: Fresh orange slices and almonds. Lunch: Grilled fish, olive oil-drizzled salad, and whole-grain bread. Snack: Cherry tomatoes with feta cheese. Dinner: Roasted eggplant, chickpeas, and couscous. 5. Keto Diet Breakfast: Bacon, eggs, and avocado slices. Snack: String cheese and olives. Lunch: Grilled salmon with a spinach salad and olive oil dressing. Snack: A handful of macadamia nuts. Dinner: Roast chicken with cauliflower mash and sautéed kale. 6. Paleo Diet Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and sweet potato hash. Snack: Fresh apple slices with almond butter. Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with olive oil and avocado. Snack: Beef jerky and a handful of walnuts. Dinner: Grilled steak, roasted vegetables, and a baked sweet potato. 7. High-Protein Diet Breakfast: Omelet with turkey, spinach, and cheese. Snack: Protein shake or a boiled egg. Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, brown rice, and green beans. Snack: Cottage cheese with cucumber slices. Dinner: Baked salmon, quinoa, and roasted Brussels sprouts.
